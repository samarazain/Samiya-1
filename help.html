Help & FAQ

What types of coffee do you serve?
We serve a wide variety of coffee including espresso, cappuccino, latte, americano, mocha, and cold brew. We also offer specialty drinks and seasonal beverages.
Do you have food options?
Yes! We offer fresh pastries, sandwiches, salads, and desserts. All our food is prepared fresh daily using high-quality ingredients.
Can I make a reservation for a group?
Absolutely! We welcome group reservations. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to arrange your group visit or special event.
Do you offer delivery services?
Yes, we offer delivery through our partner delivery services. You can also place orders for pickup through our website or by phone.
What are your COVID-19 safety measures?
We follow all local health guidelines including regular sanitization, social distancing, and mask requirements for staff. We also offer contactless payment options.
Do you have WiFi available?
Yes, we provide free WiFi for all our customers. The network name and password are displayed in our shop.
Can I bring my own cup for coffee?
Absolutely! We encourage customers to bring their own reusable cups. We offer a small discount for customers who bring their own cups to help reduce waste.
